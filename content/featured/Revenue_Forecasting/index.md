An Explainable AI model for revenue forecasting using **Temporal Fusion Transformer** and attention mechanisms. Achieved **0.83% MAPE**, **165.49 MAE**, and **329.01 RMSE** on the Walmart Store Sales dataset. Integrated **BusinessBERT** to auto-generate executive-style narratives and insights from forecasting outputs.
