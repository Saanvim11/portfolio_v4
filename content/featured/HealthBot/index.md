A multilingual SMS chatbot for preventive healthcare in rural, tribal, and semi-urban communities. Fine-tuned **MedGemma to 89.1% accuracy** with few-shot Gemini API achieving **80% multilingual** performance. Aggregates web-scraped health resources to deliver vaccination schedules, symptom awareness, and outbreak alerts.
