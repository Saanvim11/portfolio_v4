A meta-transfer framework for predictive autoscaling in serverless (FaaS) systems to eliminate cold-start latency. Combines LSTM + Random Forest with one-step meta-initialization enabling 3-shot inference without fine-tuning. Achieved **RMSE 0.117**, **R² 0.876**, and **100% scaling accuracy (±0.5)** on Huawei Cloud FaaS data.
