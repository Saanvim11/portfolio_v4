A deep learning-based plant disease detection system for **orange crop health monitoring**. Implemented and compared multiple architectures including **Simple CNN, Deeper CNN, DenseNet-style, MobileNet-style, and EfficientNet-style models** with extensive data augmentation, early stopping, and learning rate scheduling. Evaluated model performance using **accuracy curves, confusion matrices, and comparative analysis** to identify the most effective architecture for automated orange disease classification.
